Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard 
Project Overview

This project analyzes Adidas sales performance using Power BI to identify business trends, product performance, regional sales contribution, retailer performance, and profitability insights.

The dashboard helps businesses understand:

1.How sales change monthly and yearly
2.Which region generates the highest revenue
3.Which product sells the most
4.Which product gives the highest profit margin
5.Which retailer performs best
6.Which sales method is most effective

This project is useful for beginners/freshers who want to showcase data analytics, business intelligence, and dashboard-building skills.

Business Problem:

Adidas wants to improve its sales strategy by understanding:
1.Sales trends over time
2.Best-performing regions
3.Top-selling products
4.Most profitable products
5.Retailer performance
6.Effective sales channels

This dashboard provides actionable insights for decision-making.

Dashboard KPIs

✔ Total Sales
✔ Total Profit
✔ Average Profit Margin
✔ Units Sold
✔ Top Region
✔ Best Retailer
✔ Best Product
